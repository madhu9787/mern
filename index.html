Frontend development is the practice of creating the part of a website or web application that users interact with directly. It involves designing and implementing the layout, visual elements, and user experience using technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. A frontend developer ensures that a website is not only visually appealing but also responsive, fast, and accessible across different devices and browsers. They work closely with designers and backend developers to bring ideas to life, integrating interactive features such as buttons, forms, animations, and dynamic content. In essence, frontend development focuses on making digital experiences intuitive, engaging, and seamless for users.
